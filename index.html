what is Hafilaty

Our goal is to prevent any tragic incident of a child being left behind in School Bus

  • Today, a school’s primary function is not only to provide quality education to every Student, but also to ensure that every child is safe. School bus mishaps, over-Speeding and traffic woes have left schools and parents deeply concerned about the safety of their children.
  • Over the years, sadly many children have lost their lives due driver carelessness or for unfortunate reasons by being left and locked in the School Bus. There is not a bigger tragedy than losing one’s child. Such incidents greatly impact the reputation of the school.
  • We at Valuetech, felt the pain of the Parents, felt their ever growing worry, understood the difficulties and challenges faced by the Schools and with this very concern and with an aim to prevent any such unfortunate death of the little children we bring you HAFILATY
  • HAFILATY, is a comprehensive child safety platform. It leverages latest technology, coupled with a cloud enabled software platform to deliver an end-to-end school bus tracking and

We Think Innovative & Smart

Evolving out of old legacy RFID based systems, HAFILATY TM leverages exciting advancements in mobile devices. Our smart scaable - secure AdaptiBuild TM framework with GEO TrackTM enabled EXPresenseTM Technology, brings out of the box unified functions which are releveant to the school operations, not expensive and purpose built to be extremely simple to use and maintain
